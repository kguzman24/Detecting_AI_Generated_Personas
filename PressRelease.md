# AI-Generated Personas on Social Media are Growing!
## Hook
AI tools have made it easier than ever to fabricate convincing online profiles, and an average user has no way of telling the difference.

## Problem Statement
Social media is used by everyone, including children, teens, and adults. Fake AI-generated profiles are being used to scam and manipulate real people on social media everyday. This can cause serious financial and emotional harm.

## Solution
By building a dataset that combines AI face images with account behavior patterns, we can give platforms the tools they need to identify these fake personas before they cause harm to social media audiences.
